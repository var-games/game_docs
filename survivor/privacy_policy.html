Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 02, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes our policies and procedures regarding your information when you play our mobile application (the "Game"). This Game is designed to be played entirely offline.

1. Information Collection and Use

We, the developers, do not collect, store, or transmit any personally identifiable information. The Game is an offline experience. However, the Game is distributed via the Google Play Store and utilizes standard third-party services that may collect basic device data as part of their normal operation. Link to the privacy policy of third-party service providers used by the app:

  • Google Play Services

2. Log Data

If the Game crashes, standard operating system services (like Android's crash reporting) may collect Log Data. This may include your device's Internet Protocol ("IP") address, device name, operating system version, and the time and date of the crash. We do not link this data to your personal identity.

3. Local Storage (No Cookies)

Because this Game operates completely offline as a native mobile application, it does not use web "cookies." Any data related to your gameplay, such as your highest records, current wave progress, and settings, are saved locally on your device's internal memory. If you uninstall the Game or clear its app data, this locally saved progress will be permanently deleted.

4. Security

Because we do not collect or transmit your personal information to any external servers, there is no risk of your personal data being breached from our end. The security of your local game save data depends entirely on the security of your own personal device.

5. Children’s Privacy

This Game is designed to be safe and appropriate for users of all ages, including children under the age of 13. We do not explicitly collect, store, or share any personally identifiable information from any user. The Game relies solely on standard Google Play Services for basic distribution and crash reporting. If you are a parent or guardian and have concerns about the background data processed by standard Android services, please review the Google Play Services privacy policy.

6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions or suggestions about our Privacy Policy, do not hesitate to contact us at var.mobile.games@gmail.com.
