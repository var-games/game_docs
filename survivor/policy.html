2. Privacy & Data Safety

2.1 Data Collected

  • Device identifiers (Android ID, advertising ID) for analytics and debugging.
  • Crash reports (anonymized) via Firebase Crashlytics or equivalent.
  • Anonymous usage analytics (session length, performance metrics) via Firebase Analytics or similar.
  • Optional user account data (username, email, avatar) if login is enabled.

2.2 Data Usage

  • Improve game stability and performance.
  • Track feature usage and game balancing.
  • Provide customer support and troubleshooting.

2.3 Data Sharing

  • Google Play services (for in-app purchases, licensing, and cloud saves).
  • Firebase services (Analytics, Crashlytics) only in anonymized aggregated form.
  • Third-party SDKs (e.g., Unity Ads, AdMob, Play Games) with user consent included.

2.4 User Controls

  • Consent screen for analytics and personalized advertising.
  • Settings switch to opt out of non-essential analytics.
  • Delete account or data request via support email.

2.5 Compliance

  • GDPR: data subject rights, access, correction, deletion.
  • CCPA: disclosure of collected categories, right to opt out of sale.
  • Data retention policy: 12 months of inactive data then purged.

3. Age Rating & Content

  • Violence: mild to moderate non-graphic combat (fantasy/fictional enemies).
  • Language: none or mild.
  • Nudity: none.
  • Gambling: none; no real-money loot boxes.
  • Substances: none or minimal and non-promotive.
  • Online interaction: no voice chat by default; optional text chat is filtered and moderated.
  • Ads: family-safe network adherence; no unexpected interstitial content.
  • Recommended rating: Teen (13+), adjust by locale.

4. Monetization

Survivor may use one or more of the following monetization options:

  • In-app purchases (IAP): consumables (coins, supplies), non-consumables (unlockable content), and optional ad removal.
  • Ads: rewarded video ads and interstitials through Google Play billing-compliant ad networks.
  • Subscriptions: if used, clearly disclosed with renewal and cancellation policy.
  • No real-money gambling; no cash-out mechanisms.

All payments processed through Google Play Billing.

5. Prohibited & Restricted Content

  • No sexually explicit or nudity content, no pornography.
  • No hate speech, harassment, discriminatory content.
  • No unmoderated user-generated content that may violate policy.
  • No copyrighted material without permission.
  • Strict compliance with Google Play’s payment policy: no direct external payments.

6. Security & Abuse Prevention

  • Use server-side validation for multiplayer operations and save data.
  • Anti-cheat measures against speed hacks and unauthorized modifications.
  • Ownership of code and assets verified; no downloaded executable content from unknown sources.
  • Runtime permissions clearly justified, e.g., microphone or location only if used with consent and in config.

8. Google Play Submission Checklist

  1. Complete app listing metadata (title, description, graphics).
  2. Select correct category (Game > Action / Arcade).
  3. Fill out content rating questionnaire accurately.
  4. Fill out Data Safety form with real data categories and practices.
  5. Supply active HTTPS privacy policy URL.
  6. Declare advertisements and ad network usage.
  7. Configure and test in-app products in Play Console.
  8. Sign release bundle with proper keystore and upload.
  9. Run internal / closed tests for stability before production release.
